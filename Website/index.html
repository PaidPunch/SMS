How it works.

1. Purchase a Coop (seen on right).

2. Display it in your business.

3. Tell your customers to text it.

Customers get prizes.

You get advertising.

Customers receive a prize at a nearby LocalCoop business when they text your Coop.

Every time one of your customesr texts your Coop, a customer at another LocalCoop business receives your prize encouraging them to come to your business.
